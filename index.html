Smarter, Safer, and More Connected: The Future of Community Cleanup

Migrating to MongoDB: Scalable and Flexible Data Management

We are transitioning our backend database from Firebase Firestore to MongoDB, a leading NoSQL database known for its scalability and flexibility. This migration will allow us to handle a larger volume of data, including rich media and real-time interactions. MongoDB's document-oriented structure is ideal for the diverse data points we capture.

Real-Time Interactions with Redis Pub/Sub and Responsive Chat

To foster a stronger sense of community and provide immediate support, we are integrating Redis for its powerful publish/subscribe (pub/sub) capabilities and a responsive chat feature directly into the app.

Redis Pub/Sub

Enables real-time notifications and updates, such as alerts about nearby cleanup events, team activity, or important announcements. Imagine receiving a notification when other volunteers are cleaning up nearby!

Responsive Chat

Allows users to connect, coordinate cleanups, share tips, and build a supportive network. This fosters collaboration and makes the cleanup experience more engaging.

Harnessing the Power of Computer Vision and AI

Introducing groundbreaking AI-powered features for enhanced data analysis and volunteer safety.

Computer Vision for Trash Analysis

After cleanups, volunteers upload photos of collected trash. Our AI analyzes these images to:

  • Identify and Tag Trash Types: Automatically categorize litter (plastic bottles, paper, cans, etc.).
  • Quantify Trash Volume: Estimate the amount of each type of trash.
  • Generate Detailed Reports: Provide comprehensive reports for stakeholders with actionable insights.

AI-Powered Safety and Emergency Detection

We're committed to volunteer safety. AI analyzes user-uploaded photos for potential dangers or emergencies:

  • Imminent Danger Detection: Recognizes hazardous materials, injured wildlife, etc.
  • Emergency Situation Recognition: Identifies elements suggesting someone needs help.
  • Automated Alerts: Immediately notifies administrators, enabling swift communication and coordination with authorities like game and parks officials.
